# Research Breakthroughs — 2026 Radar

The most consequential AI research developments reported through mid-2026.

## 1. AI for Mathematics & Science

- **Automated Conjecture Resolution** — a proof-search reasoning agent paired with a **Lean 4** formalization agent for line-by-line machine-checked results; reported to resolve an open commutative-algebra problem with essentially no human involvement, plus new counterexamples in algebraic groups and p-adic Hodge theory.
- **AI Co-Mathematician (Google DeepMind)** — interactive multi-agent research workbench: ideation, literature search, computational exploration, theorem proving, with persistent session state.

## 2. Self-Verifying Agents

- Internal feedback loops let models **autonomously verify and correct their own work**, directly attacking the compounding-error problem that previously capped multi-step agent reliability. Widely cited as the unlock for production agents in 2026.

## 3. Reasoning at/above Human Level on Hard Benchmarks

- Frontier models now meet or exceed humans on **PhD-level science (GPQA)**, **competition mathematics**, and **multimodal reasoning** — per the Stanford AI Index 2026.

## 4. Architecture Diversification

- Beyond dense Transformers: **MoE everywhere**, **State Space Models (Mamba-2, hybrids like Jamba/Nemotron-H)**, and architecture-led efficiency gains were a headline of spring 2026 ("the frontier took a breath, architecture took the stage").

## 5. Physical AI / World Models

- World models moving from research to **standard equipment** for embodied systems; VLAs (e.g. NVIDIA GR00T N1.7) reaching open, commercially-licensed availability. (See Robotics_and_World_Models_2026.md.)
