# Agriculture And AgTech AI

This directory covers AI for agriculture, remote sensing, biomass estimation, crop yield prediction, crop disease detection, irrigation, soil analysis, weed segmentation, edge AI, and sustainable farming.

## Content Map

| Area | What belongs here |
|---|---|
| Remote sensing and earth observation | Satellite imagery, drone imagery, vegetation indices, crop classification, biomass estimation, and geospatial foundation models. |
| Crop and field intelligence | Yield prediction, crop health, growth stage recognition, disease detection, pest control, weed segmentation, irrigation optimization, and soil analysis. |
| Biomass and benchmark work | CSIRO biomass, biomass datasets, benchmark implementations, feature engineering, and transfer learning. |
| Edge and field deployment | TinyML, mobile inference, sensor fusion, edge cameras, offline deployment, and farm equipment integration. |
| Agricultural prompts | Precision agriculture, crop management, forestry, organic agriculture, permaculture, aquaculture, livestock, climate-smart agriculture, and soil prompts. |
| Sustainability and economics | Resource efficiency, climate adaptation, input optimization, carbon, biodiversity, and farm management decisions. |
