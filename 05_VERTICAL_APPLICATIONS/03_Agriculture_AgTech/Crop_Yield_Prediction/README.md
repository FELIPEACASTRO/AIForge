# Crop Yield Prediction

This directory covers AI/ML for crop yield estimation, forecasting, risk assessment, and decision support.

## Scope

- Field, county, regional, and national yield prediction.
- Satellite, weather, soil, management, genotype, phenology, market, and historical yield signals.
- Validation by geography, season, crop, lead time, and operational decision context.

## Reference Links

- USDA NASS: https://www.nass.usda.gov/
- NASA Earthdata: https://www.earthdata.nasa.gov/
