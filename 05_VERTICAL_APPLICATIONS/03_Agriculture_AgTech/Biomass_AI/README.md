# 🌾 Biomass AI

## 🌾 Biomass AI - Artificial Intelligence for Biomass Estimation

### Overview

This comprehensive collection brings together the most advanced resources for **biomass estimation using Artificial Intelligence**, covering satellite monitoring, machine learning models, precision agriculture, and carbon sequestration. Biomass estimation is crucial for crop yield prediction, forest management, carbon cycle monitoring, and sustainable agriculture.

### Why Biomass AI Matters

**Above Ground Biomass (AGB)** is a critical component of:
- 🌱 **Carbon Cycle:** Quantifying carbon sequestered by nature
- 🚜 **Crop Yields:** Predicting agricultural productivity
- 🌲 **Forest Management:** Monitoring timber production
- 💰 **Economic Impact:** Up to 10% farm profitability boost
- 🌍 **Sustainability:** Preventing overgrazing and soil degradation

**Economic Data:**
- **ROI:** $96/hectare (sheep), $52/hectare (cattle)
- **Coverage:** 380 million hectares in Australia alone
- **Global Impact:** 25% of Earth's land surface (grazing systems)
