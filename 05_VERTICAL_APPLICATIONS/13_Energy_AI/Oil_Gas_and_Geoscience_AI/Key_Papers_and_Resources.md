# Key Papers & Resources — Wellbore Geology & Geosteering AI

Curated reading list for ML applied to lithology prediction, formation-top picking, and geosteering.

## Reviews & overviews

- **AI Applications in Log Interpretation** — how AI transforms petrophysical well-log interpretation. https://ejp.researchcommons.org/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?article=1090&context=journal
- **Public Datasets for Machine Learning in Geoscience** (TDS) — FORCE, Geolink, Volve and more. https://towardsdatascience.com/public-datasets-for-machine-learning-in-geoscience-cf880862300a/
- **Deep Learning for Time Series Forecasting: Review and Applications in Geotechnics and Geosciences** — https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11831-025-10244-5

## Lithology / lithofacies classification

- **Lithology Classification Based on Well Log Data: A Benchmark for ML Models** (Mathematical Geosciences) — https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11004-026-10300-1
- **Lithofacies Prediction from Well Log Data Based on Deep Learning** (Applied Sciences, S. Sichuan case study) — https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3417/14/18/8195
- **Geological-information-driven deep learning for lithology identification from well logs** (Frontiers in Earth Science) — https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/earth-science/articles/10.3389/feart.2025.1662760/full
- **Single-well lithofacies identification based on logging response and CNN** (ScienceDirect) — https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0926985122003366
- **Applicability of deep neural networks for lithofacies classification from conventional well logs** — https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2096249524000115
- **Transforming petrophysical well-logs to images: deep learning for lithology recognition** (Earth Science Informatics) — https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12145-025-01953-3
- **Lithology Identification via Meta-Information Tensors and Quality-Aware Weighting** — https://doi.org/10.3390/bdcc10020047
