# Poker Strategy, Training & Community (Estratégia, Treinamento e Comunidade)

Curated, fact-checked guide to human poker study: training sites, books, podcasts, communities and study tools — for **research, education and off-table study only**. Every site, course, book and claim below was verified live in July 2026; unverifiable items were dropped.

---

## Responsible Gambling First (Jogo Responsável)

> **Poker is negative-sum after rake.** Most players lose long-term; even strong winners face brutal variance (downswings of tens of buy-ins are normal). Never play with money you cannot afford to lose, use strict bankroll rules (common guidance: 20–50+ buy-ins for cash, 100+ for MTTs), and treat study material as education — not a promise of profit.
>
> **If gambling stops being fun, seek free, confidential help (se o jogo deixou de ser diversão, procure ajuda gratuita e sigilosa):**
>
> | Service | Where | Contact |
> |---|---|---|
> | **CVV — Centro de Valorização da Vida** | Brazil | Call **188** (free, 24h) or chat — [cvv.org.br](https://cvv.org.br/) |
> | **Jogo Responsável (SPA / Ministério da Fazenda)** | Brazil | Official player-protection rules, self-exclusion; Portaria SPA/MF nº 1.231/2024; licensed sites use `.bet.br` — [gov.br/fazenda — Jogo Responsável](https://www.gov.br/fazenda/pt-br/composicao/orgaos/secretaria-de-premios-e-apostas/jogo-responsavel) |
> | **National Gambling Helpline (GamCare)** | UK | **0808 8020 133**, free, 24/7 — [gamcare.org.uk](https://www.gamcare.org.uk/). Note: the charity GambleAware closed on 31 March 2026 (UK moved to a statutory-levy system); GamCare has stated it intends to keep the helpline running |
> | **Gambling Therapy (Gordon Moody)** | International | Free multilingual text-based support — **Portuguese available** (native advisers + translation tools) — [gamblingtherapy.org](https://gamblingtherapy.org/) |

**Ethics & site policy (leia antes de estudar):**

- Everything on this page is for **study away from the tables**. **Bots and Real-Time Assistance (RTA) are banned by essentially every poker site.** PokerStars prohibits solvers, range calculators and solver-derived charts during play; GGPoker bans all third-party tools (RTA, bots, solvers, external HUDs). Penalties include permanent ban and confiscation of funds.
- Training-site charts and solver outputs are legal to memorize and internalize — the line is **consulting them while a real-money hand is in progress**.

## Books That Still Matter (verified editions)

| Book | Author(s) | Year | Why read it |
|---|---|---|---|
| *Harrington on Hold 'em*, Vols. I–III | Dan Harrington & Bill Robertie (Two Plus Two Publishing) | 2004 / 2005 / 2006 | The classic MTT curriculum; M-ratio and endgame play |
| *Super/System* | Doyle Brunson (+ Sklansky, Caro, Baldwin, Reese, Hawthorne chapters) | 1978 (rev. *Super System 2*, 2004) | The book that first exposed professional strategy |
| *Kill Everyone* | Lee Nelson, Tysen Streib & Steven Heston (rev. ed.; 1st ed. with Kim Lee) | 2007 (rev. 2009) | Early quantitative tournament/ICM and push-fold theory |
| *Elements of Poker* | Tommy Angelo | 2007 | Tilt, mindset and professionalism in short essays |
| *The Mental Game of Poker* (+ Vol. 2) | Jared Tendler with Barry Carter | 2011 / 2013 | Default mental-game reference; clinical, exercise-based |

*Note:* print strategy ages fast — fast-fold ("Zoom") pools, solver-era bet sizing and modern preflop theory are taught on training sites and in solver trainers, not in these books. Read the books for fundamentals and mindset.

## Brazil (Brasil) 🇧🇷

- **[BSOP — Brazilian Series of Poker](https://bsop.com.br/)** — running since **2006** (first stage: São Paulo, 67 entries at R$1,000), today the **largest live poker circuit in Latin America**. Verified **2026 calendar (six stages)**: BSOP Summer Millions @ Costa do Sauípe (23–31 Jan, 100+ tables), BSOP São Paulo @ WTC Sheraton (18–25 Mar), BSOP Winter Millions (21–31 Jul, Golden Hall WTC) and BSOP Millions @ São Paulo (13–28 Nov), among others.
- **Brazilian creators (verified active):** [AkkariTV](https://www.youtube.com/@TVAKKARIPOKER) — André Akkari, WSOP bracelet 2011, long-time PokerStars Team Pro, FURIA co-founder; [Rafael Moraes "GM_Valter"](https://www.youtube.com/@RafaelMoraesGM) — high-stakes MTT pro (US$8M+ online cashes) whose **[Como Jogar Poker](https://cjp.rafaelmoraesgm.com/)** beginner course part 1 is free.
- **Legality (not legal advice / isto não é aconselhamento jurídico):** Brazil's fixed-odds betting law (Lei nº 14.790/2023) regulates licensed betting sites, but **Portaria SPA/MF nº 1.207/2024 explicitly excludes games of skill — poker among the examples — fantasy sports and peer-to-peer games from the "online game" concept**, so poker cannot be offered under a fixed-odds betting license and its online regulatory classification remains under debate. Live poker is widely operated as a skill game (BSOP runs openly since 2006). Rules can change — check current SPA/MF ordinances before relying on any of this.
