# Predictive AI

This directory covers AI systems built to predict future events, risks, demand, behavior, outcomes, anomalies, and decisions across domains.

## Content Map

| Subarea | What belongs here |
|---|---|
| Forecasting | Time-series forecasting, demand forecasting, sales, weather, energy, traffic, and operational planning. |
| Risk prediction | Credit risk, clinical risk, fraud risk, churn, safety risk, maintenance, and reliability. |
| Classification and scoring | Propensity models, lead scoring, prioritization, ranking, and alert triage. |
| Anomaly prediction | Early warning, predictive maintenance, cyber signals, sensor anomalies, and process monitoring. |
| Decision support | Prediction plus recommended actions, confidence intervals, and business constraints. |

## Evaluation Requirements

Predictive AI pages should include target definition, prediction horizon, label delay, leakage risks, baseline, calibration, cost of false positives/negatives, and monitoring plan.
