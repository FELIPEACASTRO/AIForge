# Weather AI Platforms

Platforms, services, and codebases for AI weather forecasting, climate data access, model evaluation, and operational meteorology workflows.

## Starting Points

- [ECMWF](https://www.ecmwf.int/): numerical weather prediction and open weather datasets.
- [Copernicus Climate Data Store](https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/): climate and reanalysis data access.
- [NOAA Weather and Climate Toolkit](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/weather-climate-toolkit): visualization and export tooling.
- [Google Research WeatherBench](https://github.com/google-research/weatherbench): benchmark code and datasets.
