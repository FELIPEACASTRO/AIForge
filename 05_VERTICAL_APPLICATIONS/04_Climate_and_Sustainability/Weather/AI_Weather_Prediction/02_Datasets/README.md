# Weather AI Datasets

Curated datasets and portals for AI weather prediction, climate reanalysis, nowcasting, satellite observations, radar, numerical weather prediction outputs, and benchmark-ready time-series forecasting.

## Primary Sources

- [ECMWF ERA5](https://www.ecmwf.int/en/forecasts/dataset/ecmwf-reanalysis-v5): global atmospheric reanalysis.
- [NOAA Open Data Dissemination](https://www.noaa.gov/information-technology/open-data-dissemination): public weather and climate data.
- [NASA Earthdata](https://www.earthdata.nasa.gov/): satellite and Earth-observation data.
- [Google WeatherBench](https://github.com/google-research/weatherbench): benchmark dataset and framework for data-driven weather forecasting.
