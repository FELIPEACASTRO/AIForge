# Climate and Sustainability AI

This directory covers AI for climate science, climate risk, sustainability, emissions, energy systems, biodiversity, disaster response, adaptation, and environmental monitoring.

## Scope

- Climate modeling, weather prediction, downscaling, nowcasting, carbon accounting, renewable energy forecasting, geospatial monitoring, and risk analytics.
- Satellite, weather-station, reanalysis, emissions, ocean, land, disaster, infrastructure, and socioeconomic datasets.
- Evaluation across physical consistency, temporal generalization, spatial transfer, uncertainty, and policy relevance.

- Copernicus Climate Change Service: https://climate.copernicus.eu/
