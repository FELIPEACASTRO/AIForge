# Epidemiology AI

This directory covers AI and statistical learning for epidemiology.

## Scope

- Disease modeling, surveillance, causal inference, risk factors, forecasting, spatial analysis, and public-health decision support.
- Track population, geography, time range, case definition, confounding, reporting bias, and uncertainty.

## Reference Links

- WHO data: https://data.who.int/
- CDC data: https://data.cdc.gov/
- Our World in Data: https://ourworldindata.org/
- IHME GHDx: https://ghdx.healthdata.org/

## Routing Rules

- Put specific tracking models in `Modelagem_Rastreamento/`.
- Put public-health deployment in public-health directories.
