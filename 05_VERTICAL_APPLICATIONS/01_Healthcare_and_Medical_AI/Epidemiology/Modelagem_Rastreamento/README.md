# Epidemiology Modeling and Surveillance

This directory covers epidemiological modeling, outbreak tracking, surveillance, and AI-assisted public-health monitoring.

## Scope

- Time-series forecasting, spatial models, syndromic surveillance, contact tracing, disease spread simulation, and early warning systems.
- Track case definition, reporting lag, geography, denominator, data source, uncertainty, and public-health actionability.

## Reference Links

- WHO data: https://data.who.int/
- CDC data and statistics: https://data.cdc.gov/
- Our World in Data: https://ourworldindata.org/
- Johns Hopkins CSSE COVID-19 data: https://github.com/CSSEGISandData/COVID-19
