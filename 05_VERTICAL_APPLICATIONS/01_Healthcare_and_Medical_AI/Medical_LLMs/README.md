# Medical LLMs

This directory covers large language models and multimodal language systems for healthcare and biomedical domains.

## Scope

- Medical QA, clinical summarization, patient communication, biomedical literature, coding, triage support, retrieval, and multimodal clinical reasoning.
- Track model, license, training data, evaluation benchmark, safety restrictions, PHI handling, citations, and clinician review.
