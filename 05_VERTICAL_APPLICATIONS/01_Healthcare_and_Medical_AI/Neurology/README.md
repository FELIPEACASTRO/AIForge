# Neurology AI

This directory covers AI for neurology, neuroscience, brain imaging, neurodegenerative disease, seizure detection, stroke, brain-computer interfaces, and neurological decision support.

## Scope

- MRI, CT, EEG, MEG, EHR, wearables, speech, gait, cognition, and multimodal neurological datasets.
- Models for diagnosis support, progression prediction, lesion segmentation, seizure detection, rehabilitation, and monitoring.
- Validation across site, scanner, population, disease stage, and clinical workflow.

## Reference Links

- OpenNeuro: https://openneuro.org/
- ADNI: https://adni.loni.usc.edu/
- PhysioNet EEG resources: https://physionet.org/about/database/#eeg
- The Cancer Imaging Archive: https://www.cancerimagingarchive.net/
