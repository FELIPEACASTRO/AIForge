# Clinical Decision Support AI

This directory covers clinical decision support systems and AI-assisted clinical recommendations.

## Scope

- Alerts, reminders, guideline support, diagnostic support, treatment suggestions, risk stratification, and workflow-integrated recommendations.
- Track intended user, evidence base, action, override policy, safety, regulatory status, and monitoring.

## Reference Links

- FDA clinical decision support software guidance: https://www.fda.gov/regulatory-information/search-fda-guidance-documents/clinical-decision-support-software
- HL7 FHIR Clinical Reasoning: https://hl7.org/fhir/clinicalreasoning-module.html
- AHRQ digital healthcare standards: https://digital.ahrq.gov/standards-0
- FDA AI/ML in SaMD: https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/software-medical-device-samd/artificial-intelligence-software-medical-device

## Routing Rules

- Put CDS plus biomedical NLP in the combined CDS/NLP folder.
- Put risk prediction with explanations in explainable risk prediction.
