# ICU Monitoring AI

This directory covers intensive-care AI for continuous monitoring, risk prediction, alarm triage, sepsis prediction, ventilation support, and clinical deterioration.

## Scope

- ICU waveforms, vitals, labs, medications, notes, device streams, and EHR events.
- Track lead time, intervention window, censoring, label source, alarm burden, calibration, and bedside workflow.
