# General Healthcare AI

This directory covers healthcare AI resources that span clinical operations, patient care, medical records, digital health, population health, diagnostics, decision support, monitoring, and health-system workflows.

## Scope

- Clinical decision support, documentation, summarization, coding, triage, patient engagement, operations, and quality improvement.
- EHR/EMR, FHIR, claims, notes, labs, vitals, imaging, genomics, devices, and patient-reported data.
- Safety, equity, privacy, clinical validation, governance, and human accountability.

## Routing Rules

- Put imaging in `../Medical_Imaging/` or `../Radiology/`.
- Put medical LLMs in `../Medical_LLMs/`.
- Put clinical NLP in `../Clinical_NLP/`.
- Put genomics and drug discovery in their dedicated healthcare folders.
