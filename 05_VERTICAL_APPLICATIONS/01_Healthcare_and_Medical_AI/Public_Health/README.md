# Public Health AI

This directory covers AI for public health, population health, surveillance, prevention, resource allocation, and health policy.

## Scope

- Disease surveillance, risk mapping, outbreak detection, vaccination analytics, health equity, environmental health, public-health messaging, and intervention targeting.
- Track population, geography, denominator, data latency, bias, privacy, uncertainty, and public action.

## Reference Links

- WHO data: https://data.who.int/
- CDC data: https://data.cdc.gov/
- IHME Global Health Data Exchange: https://ghdx.healthdata.org/
- Our World in Data: https://ourworldindata.org/
