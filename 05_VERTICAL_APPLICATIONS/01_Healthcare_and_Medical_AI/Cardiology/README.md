# Cardiology AI

This directory covers AI for cardiovascular care, imaging, ECG, monitoring, risk prediction, and clinical decision support.

## Scope

- ECG analysis, echocardiography, CT/MRI cardiac imaging, arrhythmia detection, heart-failure prediction, ICU monitoring, and wearable signals.
- Track cohort, label source, clinical endpoint, temporal leakage, calibration, subgroup performance, and clinician workflow.
