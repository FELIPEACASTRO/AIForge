# Personalized Medicine AI

This directory covers AI for personalized and precision medicine.

## Scope

- Genomics, proteomics, imaging, EHR, treatment response, biomarker discovery, risk stratification, and individualized care planning.
- Track phenotype, genotype, cohort, intervention, outcome, evidence level, and clinical validation.
