# Mental Health AI

This directory covers AI for mental health support, triage, monitoring, risk assessment, digital therapeutics, and behavioral-health research.

## Scope

- Text, speech, wearable, survey, EHR, crisis, and therapy-support workflows.
- Track safety, escalation, consent, bias, privacy, clinical validation, jurisdiction, and human oversight.

## Reference Links

- WHO mental health: https://www.who.int/health-topics/mental-health
- NIMH: https://www.nimh.nih.gov/
- FDA digital health: https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/digital-health-center-excellence
- HL7 FHIR: https://hl7.org/fhir/

## Routing Rules

- Put clinical NLP in `../Clinical_NLP/`.

- Put high-risk clinical decision support in CDS directories.
