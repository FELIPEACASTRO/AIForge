# Healthcare And Medical AI

This directory covers AI for medicine, healthcare delivery, biomedical research, clinical decision support, medical imaging, genomics, drug discovery, public health, medical devices, and patient-facing workflows.

## Content Map

| Area | What belongs here |
|---|---|
| Clinical AI | Decision support, clinical NLP, clinical dialogue, medical question answering, patient monitoring, triage, and telemedicine. |
| Medical imaging | Radiology, segmentation, image synthesis, pathology, ophthalmology, dermatology, transfer learning, and foundation models for imaging. |
| Biomedical discovery | Genomics, proteomics, protein structure, molecular docking, drug discovery, clinical trials, and precision medicine. |
| Safety and regulation | AI medical devices, FDA or regulator-cleared tools, risk prediction, interpretability, bias, privacy, and deployment constraints. |
| Healthcare prompts | Diagnosis support prompts, radiology interpretation, patient education, nursing, pharmacology, mental health, trial design, and treatment planning. |
| Datasets and benchmarks | Medical image datasets, clinical notes, QA datasets, genomics, radiology benchmarks, synthetic health data, and data governance. |

## Enrichment Requirements

- Separate clinical decision support from diagnosis claims and include risk notes.
- Prefer official regulator databases, peer-reviewed papers, hospital or university sources, benchmark datasets, and model cards.
- Record whether a model or tool is research-only, clinically validated, regulator-cleared, or production deployed.
- Add privacy and governance notes for PHI, de-identification, consent, and dataset access restrictions.
