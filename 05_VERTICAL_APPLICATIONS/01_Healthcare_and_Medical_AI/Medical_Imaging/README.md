# Medical Imaging AI

This directory covers AI for radiology, pathology, ophthalmology, dermatology, ultrasound, CT, MRI, X-ray, segmentation, registration, reconstruction, and multimodal medical-image reasoning.

## Content Map

| Area | Scope |
|---|---|
| Classification and detection | Disease detection, triage, lesion detection, abnormality scoring, and screening support. |
| Segmentation | Organ, tumor, vessel, lesion, tissue, and pathology segmentation. |
| Foundation models | Medical SAM variants, radiology VLMs, pathology foundation models, self-supervised imaging models. |
| Generative imaging | Synthesis, augmentation, reconstruction, denoising, anonymization, and modality translation. |
| Clinical deployment | PACS/RIS integration, regulatory constraints, reporting workflows, QA, and monitoring. |

## Required Notes

Record modality, anatomy, dataset, label source, clinical task, metric, validation site, bias risks, and whether use is research-only or clinically cleared.
