# Drug Discovery AI

This directory covers AI for molecular design, virtual screening, docking, protein-ligand modeling, property prediction, synthesis planning, and clinical candidate prioritization.

## Content Map

| Area | Scope |
|---|---|
| Molecular representation | SMILES, graphs, 3D conformers, fingerprints, protein-ligand complexes, and embeddings. |
| Generative chemistry | Molecule generation, diffusion, reinforcement learning, constrained optimization, and retrosynthesis. |
| Structure-based discovery | Docking, binding affinity, pocket prediction, AlphaFold/RoseTTAFold-informed workflows. |
| ADMET and safety | Toxicity, solubility, metabolism, bioavailability, and off-target prediction. |
| Benchmarks and datasets | MoleculeNet, BindingDB, PDB, ChEMBL, ZINC, PubChem, and task-specific splits. |

## Enrichment Standard

Record target, assay, labels, chemical space, split method, metric, uncertainty, and experimental validation status.
