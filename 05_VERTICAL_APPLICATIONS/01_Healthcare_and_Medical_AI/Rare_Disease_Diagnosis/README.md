# Rare Disease Diagnosis AI

This directory covers AI for rare-disease discovery, diagnosis support, phenotype matching, genomic interpretation, and clinical decision support.

## Scope

- Phenotype extraction, HPO mapping, genotype-phenotype matching, case retrieval, literature grounding, and diagnostic odyssey support.
- Track rarity bias, missing phenotypes, family history, genetic evidence, source citations, and specialist review.

## Reference Links

- Orphanet: https://www.orpha.net/
- Human Phenotype Ontology: https://hpo.jax.org/
- ClinVar: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/clinvar/
- OMIM: https://www.omim.org/
- National Organization for Rare Disorders: https://rarediseases.org/

## Routing Rules

- Put genomic variant interpretation in genomics directories.
- Put clinical NLP phenotype extraction in `../Clinical_NLP/`.
