# Video Marketing Prompts

## Description
**Video Marketing Prompts** are detailed instructions provided to large language models (LLMs) or AI video generation tools to assist in the creation, optimization, and strategy of video content. They span from idea generation and complete scripts to SEO optimization (title, description, tags), the creation of thumbnail concepts, and the analysis of performance metrics. The main objective is to accelerate the video production process, ensure the relevance of the content to the target audience, and maximize the impact of the marketing campaign on platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and LinkedIn. This technique is fundamental in the era of fast, visual content marketing, allowing marketing teams to scale video production with efficiency and consistency.

## Examples
```
**1. Product Script Generation (YouTube):**
"Act as a video marketing scriptwriter. Create a detailed script for a 90-second YouTube video that promotes our new project management software, 'TaskFlow'. The target audience is project managers at small and medium-sized businesses. The goal is to generate free trial sign-ups. Include a hook in the first 5 seconds, three main benefits with storytelling, and a clear CTA for the link in the description. Provide B-roll suggestions for each scene."

**2. Video SEO Optimization (YouTube):**
"Analyze the following video title and description: [Title: 'How to use TaskFlow to manage projects']. Optimize them for the main keyword 'best project management software 2024'. Provide a new title, an optimized 300-word description, and a list of 10 relevant long-tail tags. The goal is to increase the click-through rate (CTR) and search visibility."

**3. Short Video Ideas (TikTok/Reels):**
"Generate 5 short video ideas (15-30 seconds) for TikTok/Instagram Reels for a sustainable coffee brand. The goal is to increase engagement and brand awareness among Generation Z. Each idea should include a hook, a format (e.g., 'challenge', 'quick tutorial', 'audio trend'), and a core message about sustainability."

**4. Thumbnail Concept:**
"Create 3 thumbnail design concepts for a video titled '5 Fatal Digital Marketing Mistakes'. The target audience is entrepreneurs. For each concept, describe the layout (e.g., expressive face, bold text), the color palette (e.g., high contrast, warm colors), and the main text element to maximize CTR."

**5. Post-Video Engagement Strategy:**
"Develop an engagement strategy for the comment section of a newly released video on 'Home Office Best Practices'. Create 5 open-ended questions to encourage discussion and 3 template responses for common comments, maintaining a friendly and professional tone."

**6. Video Ad Script (Meta Ads):**
"Write a 6-second video ad script for Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram) promoting an online personal finance course. The target audience is young adults in debt. The script should be direct, focused on the problem and the immediate solution, with a clear verbal CTA to 'Swipe Up'."

**7. Video Performance Analysis:**
"Interpret the following video analytics data: Average Retention Rate: 45%, Thumbnail Click-Through Rate (CTR): 6.8%, Main Drop-Off Point: 0:15 seconds. Identify the most likely problem and suggest 2 content or editing adjustments to improve retention and overall performance."
```

## Best Practices
**1. Detailed Contextualization:** Always include as much detail as possible about the target audience, the distribution platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels), the video objective (sales, engagement, awareness), and the brand's tone of voice. **2. Clear Structure:** Ask for a clear script structure, with timestamps, B-roll suggestions (supporting footage), and an explicit Call-to-Action (CTA). **3. Iteration and Refinement:** Use the initial prompt to generate a draft and then use follow-up prompts to refine specific elements, such as the hook, SEO optimization, or duration. **4. Platform Optimization:** Specify the platform's guidelines. For example, for TikTok, request a 15-30 second video with dynamic captions and trending music. **5. Use of Personas:** Ask the model to act as a specialist (e.g., "Act as a B2B marketing video scriptwriter") to obtain more focused and professional results.

## Use Cases
**1. Rapid Script Creation:** Generation of script drafts for product videos, tutorials, customer testimonials, and short ads, reducing production time by up to 70%. **2. Content Optimization for Search:** Creation of titles, descriptions, and tags optimized for SEO on platforms like YouTube, increasing organic visibility. **3. Content Idea Brainstorming:** Generation of lists of video ideas aligned with current trends, high-volume keywords, or seasonal themes. **4. Visual Element Design:** Creation of detailed concepts for thumbnails and text overlays that have been proven to increase the click-through rate (CTR). **5. Performance Analysis and Strategy:** Interpretation of video metrics (retention, click-through rate) and suggestion of strategic adjustments to improve campaign performance. **6. Editorial Calendar Planning:** Generation of a long-term video content calendar, with defined themes, formats, and platforms.

## Pitfalls
**1. Generic Prompts:** Using vague prompts like "Write a video script" results in mediocre content that is not aligned with the brand. Lack of specificity is the most common mistake. **2. Ignoring the Platform:** Creating a prompt without specifying the platform (YouTube vs. TikTok) leads to scripts with a duration and format inappropriate for the algorithm and target audience. **3. Lack of Iteration:** Treating the AI's first draft as the final product. The video marketing prompt requires refinement and follow-up prompts for optimization. **4. Exclusive Focus on the Script:** Forgetting to include crucial elements such as SEO optimization (title, tags), the thumbnail concept, and the CTA, which are vital to the video's success. **5. Data Bias:** The AI may replicate biases or content trends that are not suitable for the brand or that are already saturated in the market. Always review for originality and cultural appropriateness.
