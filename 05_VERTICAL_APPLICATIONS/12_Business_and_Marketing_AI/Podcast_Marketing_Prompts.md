# Podcast Marketing Prompts

## Description
Podcast Marketing Prompts are structured instructions provided to language models (such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude) to automate and optimize podcast promotion and growth tasks. They act as a digital marketing assistant, enabling content creators to quickly generate SEO-optimized titles, engaging descriptions, ad scripts, content repurposing plans for social media (such as Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn), guest outreach emails, and promotional newsletters. The main goal is to overcome the common challenge that "content isn't the problem, marketing is," ensuring that high-quality audio content reaches its target audience effectively and at scale. This technique is fundamental to podcast growth strategy in 2024 and 2025, focusing on efficiency and communication personalization.

## Examples
```
**1. Generating SEO-Optimized Titles:**
```
I just recorded a podcast episode about [Episode Topic/Summary]. Act as a podcast SEO expert and generate 10 title options that are catchy, optimized for keywords (include [Primary Keyword] and [Secondary Keyword]), and formatted to increase click-through rate. Include a mix of list-format titles and question-based titles.
```

**2. Creating Engaging Show Notes:**
```
You are a professional podcast copywriter. Using this summary/transcript: [Paste the Transcript or Summary], write detailed episode notes. The notes should include: (1) a strong hook intro under 80 words, (2) 5 key bullet points, (3) naturally integrated SEO keywords, and (4) 2 calls to action (subscribe + link to the product/site).
```

**3. Content Repurposing Plan for Social Media:**
```
Take this episode summary: [Insert the Summary]. Create a content repurposing plan for LinkedIn, Instagram, and TikTok. For each platform, generate: (1) 3 post ideas, (2) draft captions optimized for the platform's tone, (3) recommended hashtags, and (4) a suggestion for the type of visual clip to use.
```

**4. Guest Pitch Email Draft:**
```
I want to invite [Guest Name/Title] onto my podcast, which covers [Topic/Niche]. Write a professional and persuasive outreach email that: (1) explains why they are a perfect fit for the topic [Specific Topic], (2) highlights the value of my audience (Metrics: [Number of Downloads], [Demographics]), and (3) includes a clear CTA for scheduling. Keep it under 200 words.
```

**5. 30-Second Ad Script (Host-Read):**
```
I'm creating an ad segment for [Brand/Product/Service] that is relevant to my listeners [Audience Description]. Write a 30-second host-read ad script that is conversational and authentic. The script should: (1) start with a relatable hook, (2) explain the product's benefit in simple language, and (3) include a memorable CTA and a discount code (e.g., PODCAST10).
```

**6. Identifying Viral Clips:**
```
Using this transcript: [Paste the Transcript], identify 3 highly engaging 30-to-60-second clips that would perform well on YouTube Shorts and TikTok. For each clip, provide: (1) the approximate timestamp, (2) the hook phrase to start the clip, and (3) the reason it is engaging (e.g., controversy, insight, emotion).
```

**7. Audience Persona Development:**
```
Act as a podcast marketing strategist. My podcast is called [Podcast Name] and covers [Topics]. My target audience is [Demographic Details]. Create 3 highly detailed audience personas with names, psychographics, content consumption habits, and specific reasons why they would listen to my podcast.
```

**8. 90-Day Growth Roadmap:**
```
My podcast is about [Niche] with [Number] downloads per episode. My current challenges are [Challenges: discovery, retention, monetization]. Create a 90-day marketing growth roadmap that includes: organic promotion, collaboration/guest strategy, and SEO optimization. Provide actionable weekly tasks.
```

**9. Metrics Analysis and Optimization:**
```
Here are my podcast performance metrics from the last 3 months: [Insert Metrics: downloads, retention rate, platforms]. Act as a podcast analytics consultant and analyze what these numbers reveal about my audience and marketing strategy. Provide: (1) strengths to leverage, (2) weaknesses to improve, and (3) recommended experiments for the next 30 days.
```

**10. Interview Question Ideas:**
```
I'm going to interview [Guest Name] about [Interview Topic]. Generate 15 interview questions that are deep, engaging, and extract actionable insights for my audience [Audience Description]. The questions should be divided into 3 sections: [Section 1], [Section 2], [Section 3].

## Best Practices
**1. Detailed Contextualization:** Always include as much context as possible (podcast name, niche, target audience, episode summary, tone of voice). The more specific, the better the result. **2. Persona Definition:** Use prompts to create detailed personas before generating marketing content. This ensures the output is targeted and resonates with the ideal listener. **3. Action Focus:** Specify the desired outcome (e.g., "generate 5 SEO-optimized titles," "create a 30-second script with a CTA"). **4. Content Repurposing:** Use prompts to transform a single episode into multiple marketing assets (video clips, social media posts, newsletters). **5. Iteration and Refinement:** Use the output of the first prompt as input for a second refinement prompt (e.g., "Take title 3 and rewrite it with a more controversial tone").

## Use Cases
**1. Episode Launch Optimization:** Quickly generate all marketing assets (titles, descriptions, posts) immediately after recording an episode. **2. Audience Growth Strategy:** Create 30/60/90-day marketing plans and identify target audience personas to better direct promotion efforts. **3. Monetization and Partnerships:** Write ad scripts (host-read or sponsored) and persuasive pitch emails to attract high-profile guests and sponsors. **4. Content Repurposing:** Transform long audio episodes into short, viral clips for video platforms (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Reels) and written content for newsletters and blogs. **5. Analysis and Continuous Improvement:** Use prompts to analyze performance metrics (downloads, retention) and obtain actionable recommendations to optimize the marketing strategy.

## Pitfalls
**1. Generic Prompts:** Using vague or generic prompts without providing enough context (e.g., "Create a post about my podcast"). This results in low-quality, irrelevant content. **2. Ignoring Brand Voice:** Not instructing the model on the podcast's tone of voice and personality, leading to marketing that doesn't sound authentic to the audience. **3. Lack of Iteration:** Accepting the model's first output without refining or iterating. Podcast marketing requires nuances that AI may not capture initially. **4. Over-Reliance on SEO:** Generating titles and descriptions that are over-optimized for SEO and sound robotic or unappealing to the human listener. **5. Not Integrating the CTA:** Forgetting to include clear and specific calls to action (CTAs) in the generated marketing materials, which hinders growth and conversion. **6. Disregarding the Platform:** Using the same generated content across all social networks. Each platform (TikTok, LinkedIn, Instagram) requires a different native format and tone.
