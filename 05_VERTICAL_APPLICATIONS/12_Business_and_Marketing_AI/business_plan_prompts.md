# Business Plan Prompts

## Description
A *Prompt Engineering* technique focused on using Large Language Models (LLMs), such as ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude, to assist in creating, structuring, and detailing all the sections of a comprehensive business plan. It involves using sequential, detailed prompts to generate everything from the executive summary to financial projections and risk mitigation strategies. The effectiveness lies in iteration and providing specific context to the AI, turning the AI into a co-pilot for strategic planning. Adoption of this technique is growing, with 40% of small businesses using AI in 2024 for various tasks, including business planning.

## Examples
```
1. **Plan Outline:** "Help me outline the key components of a comprehensive business plan for my new company in the [describe your industry/niche] sector. Include purpose, target audience, SWOT analysis, marketing plan, sales strategy, offering description, financial projections, operations information, and risk mitigation. Wait for further instructions for each section."

2. **Mission, Vision, and Values:** "What are the critical questions I need to answer to define my business's mission, vision, and values? Guide me in clearly articulating these points for my business plan."

3. **Market Analysis and Persona:** "Assist me in detailing my business's target market and customer segments by asking questions about my ideal customer profile. What specific information should I include to make this section robust and informative?"

4. **SWOT Analysis:** "I want to create a SWOT analysis for my business plan. Guide me in identifying strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats by asking probing questions about each area. Finally, present a table with 5 items in each category."

5. **Marketing Strategy:** "Using the information you have about my business, service, and audience, create a detailed marketing strategy covering customer acquisition, engagement, and retention. Before you begin, ask 3 additional questions about my business so you can complete the plan."

6. **Financial Projections:** "What should I include in the financial projections section of my business plan? Help me outline the main financial statements and assumptions needed for a realistic 12-month forecast and then a high-level projection for 3 years. Ask me questions to collect information before creating this forecast."

7. **Risk Mitigation:** "Guide me in identifying the risks to my business and how to include mitigation strategies in my plan. What common risks should I consider for the type of business I am starting?"

8. **Ideation (Additional Prompt):** "Help me generate new business ideas that address current trends and solve problems in the [industry or area of interest] sector. Ideally, these ideas should be suitable for [describe your constraints – small business, limited budget, etc.]. I want to leverage my skills in [skills] and my passion for [interests]."

9. **Offering Description:** "Help me create a product or service description for my business plan that clearly conveys its value and benefits. Before creating this description, ask probing questions about my product or service to fill in the gaps."

10. **Final Consolidation:** "Present this business plan in full, in one place. Extract the elements from our dialogue, excluding our question-and-answer exchanges. My goal is to paste this information into a new document all at once."
```

## Best Practices
- **Iteration and Detailing:** Use sequential prompts, providing context and specific information at each step to refine the AI's output.
- **Human Review and Fact-Checking:** Never use the AI's output without review. Carefully verify all numbers, facts, and projections, as the AI may generate inaccurate or misleading data.
- **Comprehensive Structure:** Ensure that all the crucial components of a business plan (Purpose, Market, SWOT, Marketing, Finances, Operations, Risks) are addressed.
- **Consolidation Prompt:** Use a final prompt to have the AI compile all the generated sections into a cohesive document, removing the back-and-forth of the conversation.
- **Role Definition:** Start by defining the AI's role (e.g., "Act as an experienced business consultant...").

## Use Cases
- **Business Plan Creation:** Generation of a complete, structured business plan for startups and small businesses.
- **Idea Validation:** Testing and refining business concepts, identifying weaknesses and opportunities (SWOT).
- **Investor Preparation:** Development of financial projections and growth strategies for presentations to *stakeholders* and investors.
- **Strategic Planning:** Definition of aligned mission, vision, values, and marketing and sales strategies.
- **Risk Mitigation:** Proactive identification of risks and development of contingency plans.

## Pitfalls
- **Blind Copy-Paste:** Do not copy and paste the AI's output directly into the final document. The plan should be reviewed and personalized by a human.
- **Data Hallucinations:** The AI may generate numbers, statistics, or "facts" that seem convincing but are inaccurate or false. Fact-checking is mandatory.
- **Lack of Context:** Using generic or single-line prompts will result in a superficial plan. Detailing and iteration are essential.
- **AI Bias:** The plan may reflect biases present in the AI's training data, requiring adjustments for the real market context.
