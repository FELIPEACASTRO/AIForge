# Business And Marketing AI

This directory covers AI for marketing, growth, customer analytics, segmentation, personalization, sales enablement, content operations, pricing, and business decision support.

## Content Map

| Area | Scope |
|---|---|
| Customer intelligence | Segmentation, churn, propensity, CLV, lead scoring, and recommendation. |
| Marketing operations | Campaign planning, attribution, creative generation, A/B testing, lifecycle messaging, and automation. |
| Sales and revenue | Forecasting, pipeline analysis, pricing, qualification, account intelligence, and sales copilots. |
| Content and brand | Copy generation, SEO, social media, brand safety, creative testing, and localization. |
| Analytics and governance | Data quality, privacy, consent, measurement, uplift modeling, and experiment validity. |

## Evaluation Requirements

Track business metric, attribution logic, experiment design, privacy constraints, model drift, and whether outputs are advisory or automated.
