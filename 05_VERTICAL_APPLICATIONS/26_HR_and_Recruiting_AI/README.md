# 26 HR and Recruiting AI

> No recruiting/HR/human-resource directory anywhere in the repo. Distinct, high-deployment vertical: resume parsing & candidate matching, LLM screening, attrition/turnover prediction, skills extraction & taxonomies, and (critically) bias/fairness auditing for hiring algorithms. Strong regulatory angle (NYC Local Law 144, EU AI Act high-risk).
