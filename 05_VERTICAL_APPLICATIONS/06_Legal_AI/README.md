# Legal AI

This directory covers AI for legal research, contract analysis, e-discovery, litigation support, compliance, policy analysis, legal operations, access to justice, and legal drafting.

## Scope

- Retrieval, citation checking, summarization, clause extraction, contract comparison, case-law analysis, privilege review, and workflow automation.
- Confidentiality, professional judgment, hallucination risk, source verification, jurisdictional scope, and human attorney supervision.
- Legal datasets, benchmarks, prompt templates, evaluation rubrics, and audit trails.
