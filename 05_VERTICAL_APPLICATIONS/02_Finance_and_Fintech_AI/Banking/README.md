# 🏦 Banking AI

> The most complete index of **AI/ML for banking** — with deep coverage of **customer onboarding (KYC/eKYC)** and **banking transactions** (monitoring, AML, fraud, categorization), plus credit, payments, open banking, datasets, vendors, and regulation.

Banking is one of AI's highest-stakes verticals: every step — opening an account, verifying identity, moving money, granting credit — is now ML-assisted and tightly regulated. This section maps the whole stack.
