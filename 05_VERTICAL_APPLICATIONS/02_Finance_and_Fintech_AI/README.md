# 💰 Finance & Fintech AI

> The most complete index of **AI/ML in finance** — spanning **banking** (onboarding, transactions, fraud, AML, credit) and the **financial markets** (stocks, options, futures, bonds, FX, ETFs, crypto, quant/algo trading, portfolio, risk), plus fintech tooling, datasets, and research.

Finance is one of AI's highest-stakes, most data-rich verticals. This vertical is organized into two flagship pillars — **Banking** and **Financial Markets** — alongside focused topic folders.
