# Financial Risk Management

This directory covers AI for market, credit, liquidity, operational, model, conduct, compliance, cyber, and enterprise risk in financial institutions.

## Scope

- Stress testing, scenario analysis, early warning systems, surveillance, anomaly detection, portfolio risk, and risk reporting.
- Model governance, validation, monitoring, explainability, data quality, vendor risk, and operational controls.
- Regulatory expectations around AI, model risk, data governance, privacy, and supervision.
