# General FinTech AI

This directory covers AI resources for financial technology that do not belong only to fraud, credit scoring, trading, payments, banking, or risk management.

## Scope

- Personal finance, robo-advisory, customer support, onboarding, KYC, compliance automation, document AI, underwriting support, and product analytics.
- Banking, lending, payments, capital markets, insurance-adjacent fintech, wealth, regtech, and embedded finance.
- Governance, data privacy, explainability, operational resilience, and consumer protection.
