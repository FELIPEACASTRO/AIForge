### Overview

RAG-Anything is a state-of-the-art (2025) multimodal All-in-One RAG framework that uniformly processes and queries heterogeneous content (text, images, tables, equations) in complex documents. It uses an end-to-end multimodal pipeline, a multimodal knowledge graph, and a specialized analysis engine for visual and structured content. It has 10k stars on GitHub.

### Key Features

- **Multimodal RAG:** Uniformly processes and queries heterogeneous content.
- **End-to-End Pipeline:** Utilizes an end-to-end multimodal pipeline.
- **Knowledge Graph:** Employs a multimodal knowledge graph.
- **Specialized Analysis Engine:** Includes a specialized analysis engine for visual and structured content.

### Impact

RAG-Anything is a game-changer for the field of Retrieval-Augmented Generation. By enabling the processing and querying of heterogeneous content, it opens up a wide range of new applications for RAG, from document understanding to multimodal search.
