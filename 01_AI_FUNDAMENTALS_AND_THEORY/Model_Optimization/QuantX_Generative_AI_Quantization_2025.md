### Overview

QuantX is a 2025 research paper that proposes a hardware-aware quantization framework specifically for Generative AI workloads (LLMs, LMMs) on edge devices. It promises to be a game-changer by enabling the efficient deployment of large and complex models on resource-constrained hardware.

### Key Innovations

- **Hardware-Aware Quantization:** The framework is designed to be aware of the specific hardware constraints of the target edge device, enabling optimal quantization for that device.
- **Generative AI Focus:** It is specifically tailored for Generative AI workloads, addressing the unique challenges of quantizing these models.
- **Efficient Deployment:** Enables the deployment of large and complex models on edge devices with limited memory and computational power.

### Impact

QuantX is a game-changer for Edge AI. By making it possible to run large Generative AI models on edge devices, it opens up a wide range of new applications, from on-device personal assistants to intelligent IoT devices.
