### Overview

OpenRLHF is a high-performance, scalable, and open-source framework for Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF). Built on Ray, vLLM, ZeRO-3, and HuggingFace Transformers, it is one of the fastest RLHF frameworks available, particularly in the sample generation phase.

### Key Features

- **High Performance:** Utilizes vLLM and Auto Tensor Parallelism (AutoTP) for highly efficient sample generation.
- **Scalability:** Built on Ray, it can scale to large-scale distributed training.
- **Advanced Algorithms:** Supports a wide range of advanced RLHF algorithms, including PPO, REINFORCE++, DPO, IPO, cDPO, and KTO.
- **Active Community:** A rapidly evolving project with a large and active community (8.3k stars on GitHub).

### Impact

OpenRLHF is a game-changer for the RLHF community. By democratizing and accelerating RLHF research and application, it enables the development of more aligned and helpful AI models. Its high performance and scalability make it an essential tool for both researchers and practitioners.
