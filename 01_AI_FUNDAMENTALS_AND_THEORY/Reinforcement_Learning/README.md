# Reinforcement Learning

This directory covers reinforcement learning, decision-making under uncertainty, policy learning, value learning, model-based RL, offline RL, multi-agent RL, and RL for reasoning systems.

## Content Map

| Subdirectory | Scope |
|---|---|
| `Reasoning_RL_RLVR_GRPO/` | RL for reasoning, verifiable rewards, GRPO-style optimization, and LLM reasoning training. |

## Core Themes

- Markov decision processes, policies, rewards, value functions, exploration, and credit assignment.
- Policy gradient, actor-critic, Q-learning, model-based RL, offline RL, imitation learning, and preference optimization.
- Evaluation with environment returns, safety constraints, sample efficiency, robustness, and reproducibility.
