# Academic Prompts

This directory covers prompts for academic work: literature review, paper reading, citation mapping, research design, peer review, grant drafting, thesis support, and reproducible summaries.

## Scope

- Prompts for extracting claims, methods, datasets, limitations, citations, assumptions, and reproducibility risks.
- Academic integrity, citation accuracy, plagiarism avoidance, uncertainty labeling, and distinction between evidence and inference.
- Review rubrics for methodology, statistical validity, datasets, ethics, and novelty.
