# Legal Prompts

This directory covers general legal prompt templates for research, drafting assistance, plain-language explanation, compliance checks, and evidence organization.

## What Belongs Here

- prompt templates with role, task, context, constraints, examples, output format, and success criteria
- evaluation prompts, adversarial tests, source-citation rules, and uncertainty policies
- domain caveats, human-review gates, and version notes for reusable prompts
