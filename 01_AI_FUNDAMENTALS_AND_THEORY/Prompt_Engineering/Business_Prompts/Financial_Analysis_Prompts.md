# Financial Analysis Prompts

## Description

**Financial Analysis Prompts** are natural-language instructions designed to interact with Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI agents, directing them to perform complex finance, accounting, and planning tasks. The focus is on transforming raw data from ERP systems, spreadsheets, and reports into actionable *insights*, executive reports, and real-time financial projections. This prompt engineering technique is crucial for automating Financial Planning and Analysis (FP&A), optimizing the monthly close, managing cash flow, and ensuring compliance and audit readiness [1]. Accuracy and effectiveness depend on the clarity of the prompt, the specification of the role (e.g., "As a financial analyst..."), and the LLM's ability to integrate with enterprise data sources [2].

## Statistics

- **Predictive Accuracy:** In studies, GPT-4 demonstrated 60% accuracy in predicting the direction of future earnings, outperforming the 53% accuracy of human analysts [3].
- **Time Savings:** Leading financial firms report time savings of up to 95% on data analysis tasks when using LLMs with verified data [4].
- **Market Growth:** The global Prompt Engineering market was valued at US$222 million in 2023 and is projected to reach US$2.06 billion by 2030 (CAGR of 32.8%) [5].
- **Prompt Effectiveness:** 83.7% of respondents agree that clearer and more specific prompts lead to better AI results [6].

## Features

- **FP&A Automation:** Instant generation of forecasts, budget variance analysis, and scenario modeling (e.g., the impact of cost cuts) [1].
- **Close and Accounting:** Identifying missing transactions, incorrect vendor classification, and flux analysis for General Ledger (GL) accounts [1].
- **Treasury Management:** Real-time view of the cash position by entity and short-term liquidity re-forecasting based on recent Accounts Payable (AP) and Receivable (AR) activity [1].
- **Compliance and Audit:** Flagging journal entries without documentation and generating variance narratives for audit notes [1].
- **Predictive Analysis:** Ability to predict the direction of future earnings with greater accuracy than human analysts in some studies [3].

## Use Cases

- **FP&A and Budgeting:** Revenue forecasting, Selling, General & Administrative (SG&A) variance analysis, and *runway* extension modeling [1].
- **Working Capital Management:** Optimizing Accounts Receivable (AR) and Accounts Payable (AP), including identifying high-value invoices at risk of being late [1].
- **Executive Reporting:** Generating *burn multiple* and operating margin summaries for communication with investors and the *board* [1].
- **Due Diligence and M&A:** Rapid analysis of the financial statements of target companies and identification of accounting risks [2].
- **Investment Analysis:** Generating predictive financial factors and analyzing market trends [3].

## Integration

**Prompting Best Practices for Finance:**
1.  **Define the Role (Role-Playing):** Begin the prompt by establishing the context and the role the AI should assume (e.g., "Act as a CFO...").
2.  **Specify the Data Source:** Clearly indicate which data (ERP, NetSuite, spreadsheets) the agent should use.
3.  **Define the Output Format:** Ask for the result in a structured format (table, executive summary, chart) [1].

**Concrete Prompt Examples:**

| Category | Example Prompt | Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **FP&A** | "Compare our monthly revenue and marketing spend trends for 2025 with industry *benchmarks* and generate an executive summary." [1] | CAC Efficiency and Growth analysis for the *Board* and Investors. |
| **Treasury** | "What is our total cash position by entity, converted to USD, as of this morning?" [1] | Real-time liquidity visibility. |
| **Accounting** | "Flag any General Ledger accounts with a variance >10% compared to last month and explain the drivers." [1] | Automating Flux Analysis for the close. |
| **Risk Analysis** | "List customers with declining payment trends over the last 60 days and assign a risk rating." [1] | Early identification of potential AR defaults. |
| **Modeling** | "What is the cash flow impact if we pause G&A hiring until year-end? Project the runway extension." [1] | Strategic Scenario Modeling. |
