# Performance Review Prompts

## Description

The **Performance Review Prompts** technique refers to the use of structured and specific instructions for Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude, with the goal of generating drafts of employee performance reviews. This approach is widely adopted by managers and HR professionals to **optimize time**, **reduce unconscious bias**, and ensure that feedback is **clear, actionable, and aligned** with company objectives. Prompts are typically built on performance data (goals, 360° feedback, self-assessments) and direct the AI to produce phrases and comments focused on specific competencies, such as communication, collaboration, leadership, and goal attainment. Effective use requires providing detailed context and a final human review to ensure accuracy and empathy.

## Features

- **Review Draft Generation:** Rapid creation of initial texts for performance reviews, self-assessments, and 360° feedback.
- **Bias Reduction:** Helps mitigate recency bias, affinity bias, and gender bias by focusing language on objective behaviors and results.
- **Feedback Structuring:** Produces feedback with clear, specific, and actionable phrases, avoiding ambiguity and generalizations.
- **Alignment with Competencies:** Ability to generate comments focused on key competencies (e.g., communication, leadership, problem-solving).
- **Time Savings:** Significantly reduces the time managers spend writing reviews, allowing them to focus on discussion and development.

## Use Cases

- **People Managers:** Creating drafts of annual, semiannual, or quarterly reviews.
- **HR Professionals:** Developing standardized feedback templates aligned with company culture and competencies.
- **Self-Assessments:** Generating self-assessment drafts for employees, ensuring they cover all areas of impact.
- **360° Feedback:** Synthesizing multiple peer feedback inputs into a cohesive and constructive format.
- **Individual Development Plan (IDP):** Creating suggestions for development goals and training actions based on review feedback.

## Integration

**Prompt Examples and Best Practices (Prompting Best Practices):**

| Prompt Type | Example Prompt |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Draft Generation** | "Act as an HR manager. Write a performance review draft for [Employee Name] based on the following points: [Goal 1: Successfully completed, exceeding expectations]; [Area for Improvement: Communication in team meetings]; [360° Feedback: Strong collaborator]. The tone should be encouraging and development-focused." |
| **Competency Focus** | "Generate three constructive feedback phrases for an employee who needs to improve 'Proactivity and Initiative'. The feedback must be specific and suggest a clear next step." |
| **Bias Review** | "Analyze the following performance review draft and rewrite any phrases that seem subjective or use absolute language (e.g., 'always', 'never'), replacing them with behavior-based observations." |

**Best Practices:**
1. **Provide Detailed Context:** Include specific data (goals, projects, dates) to avoid generic feedback.
2. **Specify the Tone and Persona:** Ask the AI to assume the persona of a manager or HR professional and define the tone (e.g., encouraging, direct, development-focused).
3. **Keep the Focus on Behavior:** Ask the AI to focus on observable actions and results, not on personality traits.
4. **Human Review Is Mandatory:** The AI output should always be a draft. The final review and the injection of empathy and contextual nuance are the manager's responsibility.
