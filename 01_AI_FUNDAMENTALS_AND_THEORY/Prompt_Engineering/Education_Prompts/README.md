# Education Prompts

This directory covers prompts for teaching, tutoring, curriculum design, assessment, feedback, learning support, and educational content creation.

## Scope

- Lesson planning, formative assessment, Socratic tutoring, rubric generation, feedback, accessibility, differentiation, and study support.
- Safety for minors, privacy, academic integrity, citation, hallucination control, and teacher oversight.
