# AI / ML Communities on Discord

Curated Discord servers for Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and open-source AI. Discord renders a rich embed (title, description, image, and the repo's accent color) when an AIForge link is posted — driven by the repo's Open Graph + `theme-color` tags.

> ⚠️ Discord invite links can expire or change. When an invite is dead, search the org's official site for the current link.
