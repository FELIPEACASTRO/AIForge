# State Space Models (SSMs)

Sub-quadratic sequence models — alternatives to Transformer self-attention.

## Foundational Models

- **S4** — Gu, Goel, Ré 2021 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.00396
- **H3** — Hungry Hungry Hippos — https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.14052
- **Mamba** — Gu & Dao 2023 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.00752
- **Mamba-2** — Dao & Gu 2024 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.21060
- **Hyena Hierarchy** — Poli 2023 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.10866
- **RWKV-7** — RNN-LLM hybrid — https://github.com/BlinkDL/RWKV-LM
- **xLSTM** — Beck 2024 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.04517
- **Griffin / Hawk** (DeepMind) — https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.19427
- **DeltaNet** — https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.06484
