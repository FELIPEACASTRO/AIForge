# Small Language Models

> SLMs (sub-4B, on-device/edge) are a major 2024-2026 trend driving efficient and private AI. The repo has no dedicated section: grep for 'phi-3|phi-4|gemma-2b|smollm|qwen 0.5b' yields only 1 incidental hit (MoE README). Families like Phi-3/Phi-4, Gemma 2/3 small, SmolLM2/3, Qwen2.5-0.5B/1.5B, MobileLLM, and Llama 3.2 1B/3B deserve grouping by the SLM design philosophy (data quality, distillation, quantization for edge).
