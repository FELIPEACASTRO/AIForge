### Overview

This document covers three revolutionary advancements in Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) from 2024-2025, each addressing critical challenges in expressiveness, 3D equivariance, and intellectual property protection.

### 1. KA-GNN (Kolmogorov‚ÄìArnold Graph Neural Networks)

- **Impact:** A groundbreaking fusion of GNNs and Kolmogorov‚ÄìArnold Networks (KANs), offering enhanced expressiveness, parameter efficiency, and interpretability.
- **Innovation:** Integrates KAN modules into core GNN components, outperforming conventional GNNs in molecular modeling benchmarks.
- **Relevance:** A game-changer for drug discovery and molecular data modeling.
- **Source:** [Nature Machine Intelligence (2025)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-025-01087-7)
- **Code:** [https://github.com/LongLee220/KA-GNN](https://github.com/LongLee220/KA-GNN)

### 2. GotenNet (Geometric Tensor Network)

- **Impact:** A novel 3D Equivariant GNN architecture for modeling complex 3D molecular structures with high efficiency.
- **Innovation:** Utilizes a Geometric Tensor Network to model spatial relationships without complex irreducible representations, reducing computational overhead.
- **Relevance:** State-of-the-art performance in predicting scalar and high-degree properties on diverse molecular datasets (QM9, rMD17, MD22).
- **Source:** [ICLR 2025](https://openreview.net/forum?id=5wxCQDtbMo)
- **Code:** [https://github.com/sarpaykent/GotenNet](https://github.com/sarpaykent/GotenNet)

### 3. GNNGuard (IP Fingerprinting)

- **Impact:** A robust framework for fingerprinting GNN models to verify intellectual property (IP) and combat model piracy.
- **Innovation:** Employs a graph fingerprint construction mechanism and a robust verification module to protect valuable trained models.
- **Relevance:** Vital for the security and commercialization of Deep Learning models, addressing a critical gap in IP protection.
- **Source:** [ICLR 2025](https://openreview.net/forum?id=RNl51vzvDE)
