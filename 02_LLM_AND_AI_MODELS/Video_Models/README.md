# Video Models

This directory covers AI models for video generation, editing, understanding, captioning, retrieval, summarization, interpolation, prediction, and multimodal reasoning over time.

## Content Map

| Subdirectory | Scope |
|---|---|
| `Video_Generation/` | Text-to-video, image-to-video, video-to-video, animation, editing, controllable generation, and video diffusion models. |

## Model Families

- Text-to-video and image-to-video generation models.
- Video diffusion, transformer, autoregressive, and latent video models.
- Video-language models for captioning, QA, retrieval, and event understanding.
- World-model and simulator-style video systems.
- Video editing, inpainting, extension, interpolation, upscaling, and control models.

## Source Families

- OpenAI Sora and video-generation docs.
- Google Veo and Gemini video-related documentation.
- Runway, Stability AI, Pika, Luma, Meta, NVIDIA, Hugging Face model hubs, and Papers with Code video tasks.
- Benchmarks for temporal consistency, motion, physical plausibility, prompt adherence, safety, and watermarking.
