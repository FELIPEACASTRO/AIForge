## 1. Crop Yield Prediction

**Problem:** Uncertain agricultural productivity  
**Solution:** AI-powered yield forecasting  
**ROI:** 10% profitability boost  
**Technologies:** Satellite imagery, ML models, weather data

**Workflow:**
1. Acquire satellite imagery (Sentinel-2, Landsat)
2. Calculate vegetation indices (NDVI, EVI)
3. Integrate weather and soil data
4. Train ML model (Random Forest, DNN)
5. Predict yield at field level
6. Optimize management decisions

## 4. Pasture Management

**Problem:** Overgrazing and underutilization  
**Solution:** Real-time biomass monitoring  
**ROI:** $96/hectare (sheep), $52/hectare (cattle)

**Technologies:**
- Satellite monitoring (Farmonaut, EOSDA)
- AI models (CSIRO Image2Biomass)
- UAV/drones

**Workflow:**
1. Monitor pasture biomass daily
2. Predict growth rates
3. Optimize stocking rates
4. Prevent overgrazing
5. Maximize profitability
