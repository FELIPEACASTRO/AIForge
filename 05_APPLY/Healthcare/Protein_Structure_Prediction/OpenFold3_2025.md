### Overview

OpenFold3 is a third-generation, open-source biomolecular foundation model that aims to be a bitwise reproduction of DeepMind's AlphaFold3. It focuses on predicting the 3D structures of molecular complexes, including proteins, DNA, RNA, and ligands, with a strong emphasis on co-folding for drug design.

### Key Features

- **Open-Source Reproduction:** A community-driven effort to democratize the cutting-edge technology of AlphaFold3.
- **Biomolecular Foundation Model:** Goes beyond protein folding to predict the structures of complex molecular interactions.
- **Co-folding for Drug Design:** Specifically designed to model the interactions between proteins and small molecules (ligands), a critical capability for drug discovery.

### Impact

OpenFold3 is a game-changer for the open-source scientific community. By providing a powerful and accessible tool for biomolecular structure prediction, it accelerates research in drug discovery, materials science, and fundamental biology, fostering a more collaborative and innovative research ecosystem.
