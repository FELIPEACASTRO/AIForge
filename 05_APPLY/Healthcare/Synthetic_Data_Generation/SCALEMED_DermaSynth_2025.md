### Overview

SCALEMED is a cutting-edge 2025 framework that addresses data scarcity and privacy concerns in Medical AI by using multimodal Synthetic Data Generation (SDG). The framework uses LLMs (Gemini 2.0 Flash) to generate 1.2 million synthetic samples (DermaSynth) to train a 11B parameter vision-language model (DermatoLlama).

### Key Innovations

- **Synthetic Data Generation:** Leverages LLMs to generate a massive, high-quality synthetic dataset, overcoming the limitations of real-world data availability.
- **Low-Cost, High-Performance:** The entire project was developed for under $500, demonstrating that specialized, high-performance models can be developed with SDG for use on accessible hardware.
- **Open-Source Tools:** The project includes the open-source annotation tool **AnnotatorMed** for local clinical validation and privacy preservation.

### Impact

SCALEMED is a game-changer for Medical AI. It demonstrates that efficient, specialized models can be developed with SDG, democratizing access to high-quality medical AI and accelerating research and development in the field. The repository contains the DermaSynth dataset, the DermatoLlama model, and the SCALEMED framework.
