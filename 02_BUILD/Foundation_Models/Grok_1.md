Grok-1 is known for its unique personality and its ability to access real-time information.

### Key Features

- **Large Scale:** Grok-1 is a 314 billion parameter Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) model.
- **MoE Architecture:** Utilizes a Mixture-of-Experts architecture, which allows it to efficiently handle large amounts of data and complex tasks.
- **Real-Time Access:** Designed to access real-time information via the X platform (formerly Twitter).
- **Unique Personality:** Answers questions with a distinctive, often humorous and slightly rebellious tone.
- **Long Context Window:** Capable of processing large amounts of text for complex reasoning.

### Impact

Grok-1 represents a significant entry into the frontier of large language models, challenging existing models with its scale, unique architecture, and real-time data access capabilities.
