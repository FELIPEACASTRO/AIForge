### Overview

Gemini is a family of multimodal large language models developed by Google DeepMind. It is designed to be natively multimodal, meaning it can understand, operate across, and combine different types of information, including text, code, images, audio, and video. Gemini comes in three main versions: Ultra (most capable), Pro (best for scaling across a wide range of tasks), and Nano (most efficient for on-device tasks).

### Key Features

- **Natively Multimodal:** Built from the ground up to handle multiple modalities (text, code, image, audio, video).
- **State-of-the-Art Performance:** Achieves state-of-the-art results on numerous industry benchmarks, including MMLU (Massive Multitask Language Understanding).
- **Efficiency:** Optimized for different use cases, from large-scale data centers (Ultra) to mobile devices (Nano).
- **Long Context Window:** Capable of processing large amounts of information for complex reasoning and summarization.

### Impact

Gemini represents a major leap forward in multimodal AI, enabling more natural and capable interactions with AI systems and opening up new possibilities for complex problem-solving.
