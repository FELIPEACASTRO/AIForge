### Description

AlphaFold 3 is a groundbreaking AI model developed by Google DeepMind and Isomorphic Labs that predicts the structure and interactions of all life's molecules with unprecedented accuracy. It represents the first AI system to surpass physics-based computational tools for biomolecular structure prediction.

### Key Achievements

- **Breakthrough Prize in Life Sciences:** Awarded to Demis Hassabis and John Jumper for AlphaFold's revolutionary impact
- **20,000+ Citations:** One of the most cited AI papers in history
- **50% Improvement:** Over existing methods for protein-ligand interactions
- **2x Accuracy:** For some types of molecular interactions
- **First AI:** To outperform physics-based tools in biomolecular structure prediction

### Capabilities

AlphaFold 3 can predict the structure and interactions of:
- **Proteins:** Including complex multi-chain assemblies
- **DNA and RNA:** Genetic material structure and function
- **Ligands:** Small molecules, drugs, and chemical compounds
- **Post-translational Modifications:** Chemical modifications to proteins
- **Ions and Cofactors:** Essential molecular components

### Drug Discovery Impact

- **50% More Accurate:** Than best traditional methods on PoseBusters benchmark
- **Accelerated Discovery:** Reduces years of experimental work to minutes of computation
- **Rational Drug Design:** Enables precise targeting of disease-causing proteins
- **Antibody Design:** Optimizes therapeutic antibodies for better efficacy

### Real-World Applications

1. **Drug Discovery:** Designing new medications for cancer, Alzheimer's, infectious diseases
2. **Genomics:** Understanding genetic variations and disease mechanisms
3. **Biorenewable Materials:** Creating sustainable materials from biological sources
4. **Resilient Crops:** Engineering crops resistant to climate change and pests
5. **Enzyme Engineering:** Designing enzymes for industrial processes

### Performance Metrics

- **Protein-Ligand Interactions:** 50% improvement over existing methods
- **Antibody-Antigen Complexes:** 2x accuracy improvement
- **Nucleic Acid Interactions:** Unprecedented accuracy for DNA/RNA-protein complexes
- **Speed:** Predictions in minutes vs. months/years of experimental work
