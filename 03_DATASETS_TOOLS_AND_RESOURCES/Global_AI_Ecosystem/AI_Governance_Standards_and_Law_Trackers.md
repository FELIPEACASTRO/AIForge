# AI Governance, Standards, And Law Trackers

This file collects high-authority sources for AI governance, regulation, standards, risk management, safety, and policy tracking. Use it to expand country pages and to avoid relying on marketing summaries.

## Country Extraction Checklist

For every country page, collect:

1. Official national AI strategy, plan, law, regulator, ministry program, or digital transformation agenda.
2. OECD.AI policy rows, if available.
3. IAPP law/policy tracker status, if available.
4. Oxford Insights readiness profile.
5. IMF AIPI score/dimensions, if available.
6. UNESCO RAM participation or national assessment, if available.
7. Responsible AI benchmark status, if available.
8. Local academic/research institutions, AI labs, model/dataset projects, startup ecosystem, compute infrastructure, and public-sector deployments.

## Evidence Standard

Do not mark a country above `S1` in the coverage matrix unless at least one country-specific source has been added. Global-index inclusion alone is useful evidence, but it is not enough to claim deep country coverage.
