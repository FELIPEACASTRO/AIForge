## Coverage Semantics

- `seeded in country matrix`: the country or region already appears in `Country_And_Region_AI_Source_Coverage_Matrix_2026-07-07.md` with at least one official or high-authority aggregator source.

- LDC, LLDC, and SIDS flags come from the UN M49 table and help prioritize inclusion-focused source discovery.
