## Source Rules

- Put national AI strategies, ministry AI pages, regulator AI pages, national AI programmes, and official consultation pages in the region file for that country.
- Put OECD, UNESCO, IMF, Stanford, WIPO, EU, standards, and treaty sources in [Supranational_and_Global_Indexes](./Supranational_and_Global_Indexes/README.md), not in a single country row unless they are used as corroborating evidence.
- Keep the matrix status conservative: `S2` means official national source found; `S4` requires link dates, source freshness, and at least two reconciled source families.
- If evidence is only secondary, historical, draft-only, or unreachable, keep it as a deferred lead in the batch files instead of promoting the country.
