# Supranational And Global AI Indexes

This page stores supranational, intergovernmental, standards, and global-index sources. These sources support country discovery and corroboration, but they do not automatically promote a country to `S2` unless an official national source is also recorded in that country row.

| Source | Publisher | Scope | Use in AIForge |
|---|---|---|---|
| [European approach to artificial intelligence](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/european-approach-artificial-intelligence), [AI Act](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/regulatory-framework-ai), and [Apply AI Strategy](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/apply-ai) | European Commission | EU policy, regulation, and sectoral AI strategy | Supranational AI policy anchor, not a country row |
| [OECD.AI Policy Navigator](https://oecd.ai/en/dashboards/policy-initiatives) | OECD.AI / GPAI | AI policy initiatives from 80+ jurisdictions and organizations | Country strategy discovery and policy cross-checking |
| [Government AI Readiness Index 2025](https://oxfordinsights.com/ai-readiness/government-ai-readiness-index-2025/) | Oxford Insights | 195 governments | Country readiness comparison and S1 evidence |
| [AI Preparedness Index](https://www.imf.org/external/datamapper/datasets/AIPI) | International Monetary Fund | 174 countries/economies | Macro readiness dimensions: digital infrastructure, labor, innovation, regulation |
| [AI Index](https://hai.stanford.edu/ai-index) | Stanford HAI | Annual global AI report | Research, models, investment, education, governance, and adoption evidence |
| [Global Index on Responsible AI](https://www.global-index.ai/) | Global Center on AI Governance / partners | 138 countries and jurisdictions | Responsible AI safeguards, accountability, inclusion, and governance signals |
| [UNESCO AI Readiness Assessment Methodology](https://www.unesco.org/ethics-ai/en/ram) | UNESCO | Country assessment framework | Ethics, governance, law, data, infrastructure, skills, society, and economy readiness |
| [WIPO Technology Trends: Artificial Intelligence](https://www.wipo.int/en/web/technology-trends/artificial_intelligence/index) | WIPO | AI patent and scientific-publication landscape | Patent, publication, applicant, institution, and technology cluster discovery |
| [Global AI Law and Policy Tracker](https://iapp.org/resources/article/global-ai-legislation-tracker) | IAPP | AI legislation and policy developments | AI law and regulatory tracking |
| [Framework Convention on Artificial Intelligence](https://www.coe.int/en/web/artificial-intelligence/the-framework-convention-on-artificial-intelligence) | Council of Europe | International AI treaty | Human-rights, democracy, and rule-of-law governance baseline |
| [ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 42 Artificial Intelligence](https://www.iso.org/committee/6794475.html) | ISO / IEC | AI standardization committee | Standards taxonomy, trustworthiness, data quality, lifecycle, and AI system concepts |
