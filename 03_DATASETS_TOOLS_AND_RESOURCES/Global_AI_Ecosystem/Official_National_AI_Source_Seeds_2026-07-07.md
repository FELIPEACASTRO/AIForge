## Extraction Notes

- `S2` here means at least one official national source was found and recorded. It does not mean full ecosystem coverage.
- `EU` is intentionally stored as a supranational anchor and is not inserted into the 195-country matrix.
- Mexico is not marked `S2` in this run because the current UNESCO profile states that the country does not yet have a current national AI plan or strategy; historical strategy evidence is still useful and should be preserved.
- Next country batches should prioritize underrepresented regions: Sub-Saharan Africa, Pacific island states, Central Asia, Caribbean, and smaller European states.
