# AI Governance Datasets

This directory covers datasets for AI policy, governance, regulation, safety, standards, and public-sector AI.

## Scope

- National strategies, AI incident data, policy trackers, regulation maps, public-sector registries, standards, and institutional datasets.
- Track jurisdiction, authority, date, source type, policy status, and update cadence.

## Reference Links

- OECD AI Policy Observatory: https://oecd.ai/
- Stanford AI Index: https://aiindex.stanford.edu/
- AI Incident Database: https://incidentdatabase.ai/
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework: https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework

## Routing Rules

- Put country source seeds in the global AI ecosystem directory.
- Put broader social datasets in the parent social-science folder.
