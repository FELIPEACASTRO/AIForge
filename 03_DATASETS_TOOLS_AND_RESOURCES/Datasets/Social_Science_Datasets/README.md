# Social Science Datasets

This directory covers datasets for society-scale AI and ML research: governance, economics, public policy, surveys, demographics, institutions, behavior, labor, education, law, and cross-domain social indicators.

## Content Map

| Subdirectory | Scope |
|---|---|
| `AI_Governance/` | AI policy, regulation, governance indices, national strategy sources, safety institutions, and public-sector AI evidence. |
| `Cross_Domain/` | Social datasets that combine economics, health, education, labor, demographics, environment, or public-policy variables. |

## Source Families

- ICPSR, Harvard Dataverse, World Bank Microdata Library, OECD Data Explorer, UNESCO, ILOSTAT, World Values Survey, and national statistical offices.
- Data.gov and equivalent open-government portals for civic, economic, and administrative datasets.
- Governance trackers, policy observatories, standards bodies, and public-sector AI registries.

## Documentation Standard

Every dataset note should record provenance, license, geographic coverage, temporal coverage, collection method, unit of observation, sensitive attributes, update cadence, and known sampling bias.

## Routing Rules

- Put national AI policy source seeds in `../../Global_AI_Ecosystem/`.
- Put finance data in `../Finance_Datasets/`.
- Put climate and geospatial social indicators in `../Climate_and_Geospatial/`.
- Put healthcare datasets in `../Medical_Datasets/`.
