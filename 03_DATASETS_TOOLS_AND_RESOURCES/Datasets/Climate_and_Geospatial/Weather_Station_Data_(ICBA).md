# Weather Station Data (ICBA)

## Description
The "Weather Station Data" dataset from the International Center for Biosaline Agriculture (ICBA) is a collection of daily and hourly air temperature data collected at its weather station. The dataset is updated annually and made available in monthly files in XLSX format. It is a primary source of high-frequency climate data for the region of Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The portal also offers a version with hourly data.

## Statistics
**Versions:** Annual (2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025 - partial) and Hourly. **Format:** XLSX (monthly spreadsheets). **Size/Samples:** Not specified, but consists of approximately 365 daily records per year (or 8760 hourly records per year) for air temperature. The 2024 version, for example, contains 12 monthly files.

## Features
Air temperature data (daily and hourly). The granularity is daily for the annual versions and hourly for the specific version. The data is structured in XLSX spreadsheets, with fields such as date, time (for the hourly version), and temperature (°C).

## Use Cases
Local weather and climate modeling and forecasting. Analysis of climate trends and seasonal changes. Research in rainfed agriculture and crop resilience in saline environments. Training Machine Learning models for temperature forecasting. Energy efficiency and urban planning studies.

## Integration
The dataset can be accessed and downloaded directly from the ICBA open data portal. The data is organized by year and month. For use in Machine Learning projects or data analysis, the XLSX files should be downloaded and processed (for example, converted to CSV or a DataFrame) using libraries such as Pandas in Python.
