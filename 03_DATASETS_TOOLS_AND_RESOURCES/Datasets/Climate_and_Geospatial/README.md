# Climate And Geospatial Datasets

This directory covers datasets for climate AI, weather forecasting, remote sensing, earth observation, geospatial ML, agriculture, energy, disaster response, and sustainability.

## Dataset Families

| Family | Examples |
|---|---|
| Satellite and aerial imagery | Optical, SAR, hyperspectral, multispectral, aerial, drone, and high-resolution imagery. |
| Weather and climate | Reanalysis, forecasts, climate projections, nowcasting, precipitation, temperature, wind. |
| Land and agriculture | Land cover, crops, vegetation indices, biomass, soil, irrigation, drought, fire, deforestation. |
| Ocean and water | Sea surface temperature, ocean color, bathymetry, hydrology, flood, water quality. |
| Infrastructure and mobility | Roads, buildings, population, night lights, ports, logistics, urban form. |

## Required Metadata

Record spatial resolution, temporal resolution, coordinate system, geographic coverage, sensor source, cloud/cloud-mask handling, label source, and train/test split strategy.
