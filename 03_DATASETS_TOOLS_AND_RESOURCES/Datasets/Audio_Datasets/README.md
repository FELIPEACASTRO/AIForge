# Audio Datasets

> Curated audio datasets in AIForge spanning speech recognition, text-to-speech, voice cloning, environmental sound classification, and music analysis for training and benchmarking audio ML models.

## Contents

| Item | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [AudioSet (Google)](AudioSet_(Google).md) | Large-scale ontology of over 2 million human-labeled 10-second YouTube audio clips across 600+ sound event classes. |
| [CSTR VCTK Corpus (version 0.92)](CSTR_VCTK_Corpus_English_Multi-speaker_Corpus_for_CSTR_Voice_Cloning_Toolkit_(version_0.92).md) | English multi-speaker corpus for the CSTR Voice Cloning Toolkit, widely used for TTS and voice cloning. |
| [Common Voice (Mozilla)](Common_Voice_(Mozilla).md) | Mozilla's massively multilingual crowd-sourced speech dataset for open ASR and speech technology. |
| [ESC-50](ESC-50_(Environmental_Sound_Classification).md) | Benchmark of 2,000 labeled environmental audio recordings across 50 classes for sound classification. |
| [LJSpeech Dataset](LJSpeech_Dataset.md) | Single-speaker English read-speech corpus, a standard benchmark for neural text-to-speech synthesis. |
| [LibriSpeech ASR Corpus](LibriSpeech_ASR_Corpus.md) | ~1,000 hours of read English audiobook speech, a core benchmark for automatic speech recognition. |
| [MusicNet](MusicNet.md) | Collection of classical music recordings with aligned note-level annotations for music information retrieval. |
| [TIMIT Acoustic-Phonetic Continuous Speech Corpus](TIMIT_Acoustic-Phonetic_Continuous_Speech_Corpus.md) | Phonetically transcribed American English speech corpus for acoustic-phonetic and ASR research. |
| [UrbanSound8K](UrbanSound8K.md) | 8,732 labeled urban sound excerpts across 10 classes for environmental sound classification. |
