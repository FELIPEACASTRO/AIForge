# Medical Datasets

This directory covers medical and healthcare datasets for imaging, clinical text, genomics, medical QA, public health, drug discovery, and patient monitoring.

## Dataset Families

| Family | Examples |
|---|---|
| Medical imaging | X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, pathology, ophthalmology, dermatology, segmentation masks. |
| Clinical text | Notes, discharge summaries, EHR-derived corpora, de-identified records, medical QA. |
| Biomedical research | Genomics, proteomics, multi-omics, drug response, molecular docking, protein datasets. |
| Public health | Epidemiology, surveillance, claims, registries, population health, social determinants. |
| Synthetic health data | Synthea-style generated records, synthetic images, privacy-preserving benchmarks. |

## Governance Notes

Medical datasets require special handling: record license, consent, PHI/de-identification status, jurisdiction, approved use, access process, and clinical validation limits.
